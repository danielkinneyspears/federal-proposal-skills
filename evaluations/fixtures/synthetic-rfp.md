## Solicitation SYN-2026-R-0001

**Agency:** Federal Synthetic Services Administration (FSSA), Office of Digital
Operations
**Title:** Enterprise IT Service Desk Support
**Contract type:** Firm-Fixed-Price
**Set-aside:** Total small business set-aside (NAICS 541519, size standard
applies)
**Period of performance:** One 12-month base period plus four 12-month option
periods
**Proposal due:** 2:00 PM Eastern Time, 45 days after release, via the FSSA
eProcurement portal

---

## Section C — Performance Work Statement (summary)

The contractor shall operate the FSSA enterprise IT service desk supporting
approximately 9,000 users across three sites.

- **C.1** The contractor shall provide Tier 1 and Tier 2 service desk support
  during the hours 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM Eastern, Monday through Friday.
- **C.2** The contractor shall resolve 80% of Tier 1 tickets at first contact.
- **C.3** The contractor shall acknowledge all incidents within 15 minutes of
  receipt and provide status updates every 30 minutes for severity-1 incidents.
- **C.4** The contractor shall provide a transition plan and complete transition
  within 30 calendar days of contract award with no interruption of service.
- **C.5** The contractor shall maintain a quality control program and report
  performance against the standards in the Quality Assurance Surveillance Plan
  monthly.
- **C.6** The contractor shall provide a Program Manager and a Service Desk Lead
  as key personnel.

---

## Section L — Instructions to Offerors

**L.1 General.** The proposal shall consist of three separately bound volumes:
Volume I — Technical, Volume II — Past Performance, Volume III — Price. Price
information shall appear only in Volume III.

**L.2 Format.** All volumes shall use 12-point Times New Roman, 1-inch margins,
and single spacing. Pages shall be 8.5 x 11 inches. Each volume shall be
submitted as a separate searchable PDF named `SYN-2026-R-0001_<VolumeName>.pdf`.

**L.3 Volume I — Technical (page limit: 25 pages).** The Technical Volume shall
address, in this order:
- L.3.1 Technical Approach. Describe the approach to providing Tier 1 and Tier 2
  service desk support and to meeting the first-contact resolution and incident
  response standards.
- L.3.2 Transition Approach. Describe the approach to completing transition
  within 30 days without service interruption.
- L.3.3 Management Approach. Describe the management structure, including the
  role of the Program Manager.
- L.3.4 Quality Control. Describe the quality control program.
- L.3.5 Key Personnel. Provide a resume, not to exceed two pages each, for the
  proposed Program Manager and Service Desk Lead. Resumes do not count against
  the 25-page limit.

**L.4 Volume II — Past Performance.** Provide three past performance references
for contracts performed within the last five years, each not to exceed three
pages.

**L.5 Volume III — Price.** Provide a completed price schedule for the base and
all option periods.

---

## Section M — Evaluation Factors for Award

**M.1 Basis for award.** Award will be made to the offeror whose proposal
represents the best value to the Government, considering the factors below.
Non-price factors, when combined, are significantly more important than price.

**M.2 Factor 1 — Technical Approach.** The Government will evaluate the
soundness of the proposed approach to service desk support, transition, and
management, and the degree to which it reduces performance risk.

**M.3 Factor 2 — Past Performance.** The Government will evaluate the recency,
relevancy, and quality of the offeror's past performance and assign a
performance confidence rating.

**M.4 Factor 3 — Price.** The Government will evaluate the total proposed price
for the base and all option periods for reasonableness.

Factor 1 is more important than Factor 2. Factors 1 and 2 combined are
significantly more important than Price.
